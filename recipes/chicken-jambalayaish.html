Chicken, Chilli & Black Pudding
Jambalaya-ish Rice

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast or thighs, chopped
  • Black pudding (diced small)
  • Onion, celery, pepper (the holy trinity)
  • Garlic
  • Mixed fresh chillies (early + late additions)
  • Smoked paprika
  • Rice
  • Chicken stock
  • Oil + butter
  • Salt, pepper, MSG (optional but powerful)
  • Vinegar or citrus to finish

Method

  1. Fry the black pudding gently in oil until crisp. Remove and reserve.
  2. Soften onion, celery and pepper in the same pan until sweet.
  3. Add garlic, smoked paprika and early chillies. Cook briefly.
  4. Add chicken and cook until just sealed.
  5. Stir in rice, then add stock to just cover. Simmer gently.
  6. Add late chillies and adjust seasoning as the rice finishes.
  7. Finish with a knob of butter and a dash of acid.
  8. Fold through the crispy black pudding at the last moment.
